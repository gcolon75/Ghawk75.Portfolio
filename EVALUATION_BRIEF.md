## What's Blocking Interviews ❌

### 1. **Ambiguous Positioning (Critical)**
**Issue:** Hero says "exploring creativity" instead of "I'm a [specific role]"

**Why It Matters:** Recruiters spend 5-10 seconds deciding relevance. Poetic language = instant pass.

**Fix:** Lead with job title + specialty
- Example: "Systems Designer & Game Mechanics Engineer | Building tactical depth into roguelike experiences"
- **Effort:** 1 hour
- **Impact:** +25-30% conversion

---

### 2. **Missing Impact Metrics (Critical)**
**Issue:** Projects describe approach but lack scale/outcome indicators

**Why It Matters:** "I built X" is table stakes. "I built X that handled Y scale" is differentiating.

**Fix:** Add directional metrics to top 3 projects
- Tower of Greed: "50+ page design doc, 3 iteration cycles"
- Amadeus: "Reduced alert noise by ~60%, 99.9% uptime"
- Axle: "25+ high-fidelity screens, validated with 8 user interviews"
- **Effort:** 2-3 hours
- **Impact:** +20-25% conversion

---

### 3. **Hidden Credentials (High Priority)**
**Issue:** UCSD degree only in structured data, not visible to recruiters

**Why It Matters:** Education is a primary screening filter. If recruiters can't see it in 30 seconds, it doesn't exist.

**Fix:** Add Education section to About page
- **Effort:** 30 minutes
- **Impact:** +10-15% conversion

---

### 4. **Generalist vs. Specialist Tension (High Priority)**
**Issue:** 8 projects across 4 disciplines creates "jack of all trades" perception

**Why It Matters:** Most roles seek specialists with strategic breadth, not generalists.

**Fix:** Tier projects into "Core Expertise" (3-4 best) vs "Demonstrating Range"
- **Effort:** 1 hour
- **Impact:** Clarifies positioning without hiding versatility

---

### 5. **No Collaboration Evidence (Medium Priority)**
**Issue:** Missing team sizes, stakeholder context, cross-functional work

**Why It Matters:** Hiring managers want to know you can work in teams and navigate complexity.

**Fix:** Add collaboration context to each project
- Example: "Sole designer working with 2 engineers and 1 PM, presented to C-suite"
- **Effort:** 2 hours
- **Impact:** Addresses team fit questions
